## Why Consider This?

### Pros
- **Differentiation** - No loyalty platform does this
- **Viral potential** - Crypto/meme coin culture is shareable
- **Young demographic** - Appeals to Gen Z/Millennials
- **Press/buzz** - "First loyalty program with its own token"
- **Brand fit** - Pirate theme + crypto = natural match

### Cons/Risks
- Regulatory complexity (securities law, money transmission)
- Volatility if token has market price
- Technical complexity (wallet integration)
- Distraction from core product
- Potential reputational risk if token fails

## Implementation Options

### Option A: Simple Cash Out
- Points ‚Üí USD via PayPal/Venmo
- Low regulatory burden
- Easy to implement

### Option B: Stablecoin Rewards
- Points ‚Üí USDC
- Partner with crypto on-ramp (Coinbase, etc.)
- Medium complexity

### Option C: Full $PIRATE Token
- Create token on Solana or Base (low fees)
- Build tokenomics model
- Legal review required
- High complexity, high reward
