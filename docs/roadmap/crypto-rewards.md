## Decision Summary

**We are NOT pursuing cryptocurrency features at this time.**

After thorough analysis, the regulatory complexity, implementation cost, and user friction outweigh the novelty benefits. We can achieve similar engagement goals through traditional gamification mechanics.

---

## Rationale

### Primary Concerns

| Issue | Impact |
|-------|--------|
| **Regulatory complexity** | GENIUS Act (Jan 2025), SEC classification uncertainty, state-by-state money transmitter licensing |
| **User friction** | Wallet setup, gas fees, key management, seed phrase education |
| **Volatility risk** | Token/reward value unpredictability damages trust |
| **Implementation cost** | Blockchain infrastructure, security audits, compliance overhead |
| **Target market fit** | Local coffee shops/restaurants don't need crypto complexity |

### What We're Doing Instead

The [Gamification v2 system](gamification-v2-overview.md) achieves similar engagement through:

- **Collection mechanics** - Pokemon Go-style captures create collectibility without blockchain
- **XP & Ranks** - Platform progression drives retention
- **Crews & Leaderboards** - Social competition creates stickiness
- **Predictions** - Free-to-play Oracle's Challenge adds game layer
- **Bounties & Events** - Tenant-driven engagement
