## Two-Step vs One-Step Claiming

### Option A: Two-Step (Claim then Redeem) - Recommended

```
Points Balance (125)
    â”‚
    â–¼ Tap "Claim"
Points Deducted (now 25)
Reward moved to "My Rewards"
    â”‚
    â–¼ At restaurant, show QR
Staff redeems
Reward marked as "Used"
```

**Pros:**
- Customer has "inventory" of rewards
- Can claim when they hit threshold
- Creates anticipation
- Clear ownership

**Cons:**
- Expiration risk
- Points "locked" in claimed rewards

### Option B: One-Step (Instant Redeem)

```
Points Balance (125)
    â”‚
    â–¼ At restaurant, tap "Use Now"
Points Deducted & Staff notified
Instant redemption
```

**Pros:**
- No expiration risk
- Points stay liquid longer

**Cons:**
- Less "tangible" reward feeling
- No anticipation/planning

### Recommendation: Two-Step with Flexibility

- Default to two-step (claim â†’ redeem)
- Allow tenant to configure one-step if preferred
- Allow "instant redeem" for customers who prefer it
