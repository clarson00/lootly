## Fulfillment

### Options

| Provider | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| **Print-on-demand** (Printful, Printify) | No inventory, automated | Higher per-unit cost |
| **Bulk order** (Custom Ink) | Lower cost | Inventory risk |
| **Hybrid** | Best of both | More complex |

### Recommendation: Print-on-Demand for MVP

- No upfront inventory cost
- Automated fulfillment
- Scale as needed
- Add bulk ordering later for popular items
