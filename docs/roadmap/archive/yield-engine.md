### Model A: Customer Passive Yield

**How it works:**
- Every customer with a balance earns proportional yield
- Monthly distribution based on average balance held
- "Your doubloons grew by X this month"

**Example:**
```
Customer has 500 doubloons (average balance)
```

**Why it works:**

- Reason to stay in the program even during slow periods

## REGULATORY CONSIDERATIONS

### The GENIUS Act (US, July 2025)

- Prohibits yield-bearing stablecoins for US retail users
- BUT: We're not giving users stablecoins
- We're giving users "loyalty points" that happen to grow
- The yield-bearing assets are held by RewardsPirate (business), not consumers

### Structure Options

**Option A: RewardsPirate Holds Everything**
- We hold USDC/yield assets in corporate treasury
- We distribute "bonus points" (not yield) to customers
- Points are not securities, not stablecoins
- Clean separation

**Option B: Business Treasury Model**
- Each business's subscription goes to their own yield pool
- They earn yield on their own money
- They distribute as bonus points to their customers
- RewardsPirate is just the platform

**Option C: Network DAO (Future)**
- Decentralized treasury
- Token governance
- Community-owned yield
- More complex, more regulatory questions

**Recommendation: Start with Option A. Cleanest, simplest, most controllable.**
